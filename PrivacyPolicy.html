Privacy Policy for SnapRetro

📌 Overview

SnapRetro is a photo editing application that allows applying filters, effects, textures, and templates to photos. The app follows a privacy-first approach, meaning all data stays on the device.

📷 Information

Photos and Media

  • No collection or storage of photos
  • No uploading of images
  • All processing happens locally on the device

Permissions

  • Photos / Media Access – used to import and save images

🔐 Usage

No personal data is collected.

  • Editing images within the app
  • Saving processed images to the device

🚫 Data Sharing

  • No data shared with third parties
  • No analytics tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No external servers used

🔒 Storage

All data is stored locally on the device only.

  • No server storage
  • No uploads
  • No user tracking

👶 Children's Privacy

No personal information is collected from children under 13. The app is suitable for general audiences.

🌍 Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used.

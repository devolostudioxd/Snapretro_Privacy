📸 Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: July 2026 🔒 privacy-first · local only

SnapRetro respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application SnapRetro handles your information.

📌 Overview

SnapRetro is a photo editing application that allows users to apply filters, effects, textures, and templates to their photos. The app is designed with a privacy-first approach, meaning your data stays on your device.

📷 Information We Collect

1. Photos and Media (User-Provided Content)

SnapRetro may access photos stored on your device only when you choose to import or save images.

  • No collection, storage, or uploading of your photos
  • All image processing happens locally on your device

2. Device Permissions

SnapRetro may request the following permission:

  • Photos / Media Access
    • Used to import images for editing
    • Used to save edited images to your gallery

Access to media happens only with your explicit action.

🔐 How Information Is Used

No personal data is collected.

Access to photos is strictly limited to:

  • Editing images within the app
  • Saving processed images back to your device

🚫 Data Sharing

No user data is shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

  • No analytics tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No external servers involved

🔒 Data Storage

All data is processed and stored locally on your device only.

The app does not:

  • Store data on servers
  • Upload images
  • Track user activity

👶 Children's Privacy

SnapRetro does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since no data is collected, the app is suitable for general audiences.

🌍 Third-Party Services

SnapRetro does not integrate third-party services that collect user data.
